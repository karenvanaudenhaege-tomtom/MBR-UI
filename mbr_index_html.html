Feedback resolution
Prevented bad edits reached 68.7% in January (target ≥50%), up significantly from 35.6% in December, partly due to a new Ground Truth source (OSM Data Working Group) showing quarantine performs better than initially thought.
Entity matching
8× duplicate reduction with 96% matching quality for Shops, Restaurants, and Nightlife POI groups. Rolling out to TOP29 priority countries. Work started on 8 new POI category groups.
HDT 1 — HD product lines
Multiple NDS.Live variants discovered — not all manageable on NDS.Live side. At least 2–3 product lines anticipated based on customer road class coverage requirements. Not budgeted. Clear agreements needed between HD team, Platform, and NDS.Live to avoid per-customer proliferation. Decision required this cycle.
HDT 2 — Map evaluation tool (Vertex)
Prospects using Vertex as improvised evaluation tool. DiDi and Audi flagged POI search/display gaps (stacked POIs aggregated, search latency). UXR research initiated. If Vertex becomes the long-term answer, roadmap and budget amendments required. H1 focus remains OPS efficiency — short-term Vertex improvements not prioritised. Business case targeted for Q6.
HDT 3 (update) — GEMS
Microsoft expressed alignment with commercial-first GEMS pitch. Next steps: collaborate with POI team on business drivers, present detailed proposal to Microsoft in early January. Potential outcomes: open-source, commercial, or hybrid.
HDT 4 (update) — Amazon Last Mile
POC results shared with ALM: 95% accuracy, 99.5% precision. ALM expressed interest in joint workshops to explore a North Star. TomTom preparing workshop scope and strategy. Commercial proposal to follow.
Risk 1 — Honda HD measurements
Commitment to produce HD measurements for Honda using AllVision data is at risk due to mismatch between Genesis HD and reference data. Deadline extended to end of February. Mitigation: engaging AllVision this week to resolve differences. Owner: Platform/HD team.
Risk 2 — South Korea MCC
Map Certification Code at risk for ONKOR_25050. Features in sensitive area polygons must be removed urgently. Value streams lack tools to filter/delete these features. Removal plan being compiled; effort estimations underway across value streams and platform. Staff engineers from multiple teams to propose solutions.
Risk 3 — Vertex evaluation gaps (ongoing)
Vertex not set up as a customer evaluation tool. Short-term improvements not on H1 roadmap (OPS efficiency focus). Discovery and research underway with UXR. Business case for Vertex-as-evaluation-tool targeted Q6.
Microsoft
First Customer Feedback Hub demo completed — well received. Feature requests raised: (1) show where fix was applied (OSM vs TomTom), (2) context on rejections, (3) analytics on slow-resolution feedback. SLA proposal (high-priority feedback volumes + best-effort long-tail) under review, to be concluded early January. Sample bulk feedback from Microsoft incoming.
New prospects
Justos signed extended POC contract for the analytics engine. e-smart is a new POC partner for BYOD/MMaaS — early stage, strong fit with product vision, high transparency. Amazon Last Mile workshops being scoped following strong POC results.
Toyota ADAS (internal)
Crosslink editing: validation layer set up in Vertex Orbis Staging; fallout loaded as tasks in Issue Tracker; end-to-end editing pipeline test in progress. Lanes editing: locking geometry by default, auto-refresh after changelet submission. Efficiency reached 7.5 tasks/hr (up from 4 tasks/hr).
